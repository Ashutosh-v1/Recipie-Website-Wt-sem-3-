Caramel Cold Coffee
Ingredients:
  • COffee coco powder
  • milk, as per requirement
  • choclate syrup
  • shaker
Instructions:
  1. create a sort of foam with coco powder in a bowl
  2. mix it well untill foam appears
  3. then add the milk in the shaker
  4. combine milk and foam of coco powder in the shaker and add choclate syrup
  5. Serve the caramel coffee
